About

Greetings! My name is Vadym Hulchenko.
Transportation Logistics professional in the past, who plunged into the universe of coding in mid 2020 to expand my passion for creativity and technology. Despite of my lifetime attachment to computers, I did not realize how much I would love coding! My formal background has given me the strong skill of problem solving, increasing my performance in the diverse world of technology. Coding is an art form which interferes with everyone’s daily life in one way or another, and I am thrilled to take part in the creative process.
In my spare time, you can find me exploring new ideas on my computer, gaming, or in the Rocky Mountains with my fiancée, Bailey, and our dog, Tony.
